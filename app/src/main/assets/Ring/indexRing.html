HOW TO PLAY RING OF FIRE

You will need...

* A pack of cards

* A medium sized cup

* Something to drink

Step 1:

Put the mug in the middle and form a ring of cards around ( just as it shows on the image below). Before that you´ll obviously need to shuffle the cards. Keep in mind that the ring is suppose to stay whole as long as possible. The clumsy on who managaes to break the ring will take a shot as a punishment and after that fix the cards so it builds a whole ring again. This will continue till it will be impossible to make a ring anymore.

Step 2:

The group decides who goes first to pick up the card and the app is provided with rules that will help you follow the game much easier. Remember that in this version of the game it doesn´t matter which color or which symbol the card have. For example Hearst two would be the same as Spades two.
